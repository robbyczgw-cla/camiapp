## Inspiration

The idea for CamiApp was directly inspired by [expo-openclaw-chat](https://github.com/brunobar79/expo-openclaw-chat) by Bruno Barbieri, which demonstrated that a native OpenClaw chat experience via Expo was possible and provided the SDK to make it happen.
